# Programs, contacts, and the AI Ambassador Forum

## The Enterprise AI team
The Enterprise AI team owns the Forcepoint Intelligence Platform and the
Enterprise AI portal. Contact them at ITEnterpriseAIteam@forcepoint.com for help
with AI tools, skills, policy questions, and tool approvals.

## The Enterprise AI portal
The Forcepoint Enterprise AI portal is the dedicated home for everything AI at
Forcepoint, replacing SharePoint as the central hub. It hosts how-to guides,
shared prompts, the skills library, news, events, and the Ask AI assistant.

## AI Ambassador Forum
The AI Ambassador program helps each department adopt AI effectively and safely.
The Jira and Confluence operating model for the Ambassador Forum is live and
covers all six department boards plus the central platform space. Ambassadors are
a good first point of contact within your own department.

## Events and enablement
The portal runs enablement sessions such as the "Skills 101" brown-bag, a
hands-on introduction to authoring a Forcepoint Enterprise AI skill end-to-end in
under an hour. Check the Events section of the portal for upcoming sessions and
registration.
