# Getting started with Claude at Forcepoint

## Writing your first prompt
Be specific about your role, what you need, and the format you want. Claude
responds to context — the more relevant detail you give, the better the output.
Start by stating who you are and the outcome you want, then provide the material
and ask for a specific format (a list, a table, a short summary).

## Summarising long documents
Paste a document and ask Claude to extract the key decisions, flag risks, or
rewrite it for a specific audience. This works best with reports, policies, and
contracts. Ask for the top three actions or risks if you need an executive view.

## Multi-step workflows with Claude Projects
Store context, instructions, and knowledge in a Claude Project so Claude
understands your team's work without you repeating yourself every session.
Projects are useful for recurring workflows such as sales outreach review or
incident summarisation where the same background applies to many tasks.

## Safe use reminder
Whatever the task, do not paste Protected Information, source code, customer PII,
or Forcepoint Confidential data. See the Forcepoint AI Policy (FP-IS-AI).
