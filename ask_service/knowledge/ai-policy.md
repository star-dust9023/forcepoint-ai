# Forcepoint AI Policy (FP-IS-AI)

## What the policy is
Forcepoint AI Policy FP-IS-AI governs how all personnel may use AI tools for
work purposes. It was updated to v1 and is in effect as of 1 April 2026. All
personnel are required to comply. Review the policy before using any AI utility
for work.

## What you must never share with AI tools
Never input Protected Information, source code, customer PII (personally
identifiable information), or Forcepoint Confidential data into any AI tool —
including external chat assistants. Check the AI policy before you start any
task that involves pasting company or customer data.

## Tool approvals and the AI Registry
Before using a new AI tool for work, confirm it is approved in the AI Registry.
The Registry records which tools have been reviewed and approved for which
purposes. If a tool is not listed, treat it as unapproved and ask the Enterprise
AI team before using it with any work content.

## Where to get help
For questions about the policy, tool approvals, or safe AI use, contact the
Enterprise AI team at ITEnterpriseAIteam@forcepoint.com. The AI Ambassador
program can also help your department adopt AI safely.
